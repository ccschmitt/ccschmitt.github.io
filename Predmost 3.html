Predmost 3

Predmost 3 is an archaeological find from central Europe in the Czech republic, and is geologically dated as Late Pleistocene. The find is a skull found in Predmosti, Moravia, and dated to 26,000yB.P. and has a cranial capacity of 1580 cubic centimetres (Holloway 2000;Holloway et al 2004). The skull was destroyed in the Second World War by Nazi Germany.
