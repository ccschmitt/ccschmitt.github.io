Yamashita-Cho Man

The Yamashita Cave People are the prehistoric humans known from many bones found in the Yamashita limestone cave near Naha, in Okinawa, Japan. The remains have been dated at 32,000Â±1000 years ago. The most important bones found in the cave in Yamashita are those of an approximately 6 to 8-year-old girl.
