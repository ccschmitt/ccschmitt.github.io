Ndutu

The Ndutu skull is the partial cranium of a Middle Pleistocene hominin found at Lake Ndutu in northern Tanzania.

For more information, click here. Or, Try these sources:

  • Clarke, R J (August 1976). “New cranium of Homo erectus from Lake Ndutu, Tanzania”. Nature 262: 485-487.
